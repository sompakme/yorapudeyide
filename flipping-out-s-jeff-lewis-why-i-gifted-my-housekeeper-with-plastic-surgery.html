Flipping Out's Jeff Lewis: Why I Gifted My Housekeeper With Plastic Surgery

Jeff Lewis may not win any Boss of the Year awards, but the Flipping Out reality star sure knows how to give a gift worth talking about.

For his live-in housekeeper Zoila Chavez's birthday this year, 40-year-old Lewis presented her with a present to end all presents: Plastic surgery!

"I gave her the option of having an elective surgery procedure," Lewis tells Us Weekly of how he celebrated his unflappable Nicaraguan maid's special day. "It ended up being seven, actually. "I just adore Zoila, so it's my way of saying thank you."

A stark contrast from his 2009 gift of a Mini Cooper, Chavez's surgery has been kept well under wraps and will unfold over the next handful of episodes, starting with Tuesday's surgical consultation.

Remaining tight-lipped on the exact procedures Chavez received, Lewis tells Us he looked to Hollywood when evaluating her possible enhancements.

"I suggested breast implants. And I really like Coco [Austin] from Ice Loves Coco. I love her breasts. I think that they would be good on Zoila," he tells Us. "But it might be hard to clean [and] hard to move that vacuum!"
