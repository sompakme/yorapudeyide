FAQ

What is the AACA Forum?

The AACA Forum is an online community for antique automobile enthusiasts. It provides a platform for discussions, technical support, vehicle identification, photo and video sharing, car appreciation, automotive legislation discussions, and buying/selling automobiles and parts.

What can I discuss on the AACA Forum?

You can engage in conversations about antique automobiles, share experiences, ask questions, seek advice, troubleshoot technical issues, identify vehicles or parts, showcase photos and videos, appreciate unrestored cars, discuss automotive legislation, and connect with other enthusiasts for buying or selling antique automobiles and parts.

How can I get technical support on the AACA Forum?

In the Technical Support section, post specific details about your antique automobile, such as the year, make, model, and the technical issue you need help with. Members of the community will offer guidance and advice to help you troubleshoot and repair your classic vehicle.

How can I identify a vehicle or part on the AACA Forum?

In the Vehicle Identification category, you can post photos of vehicles or parts you want to identify. The knowledgeable community members can help you determine the make, model, or provide any other relevant information.

Can I share photos and videos on the AACA Forum?

Yes, the Photos and Videos section allows you to share your love for antique automobiles visually. You can post photos and videos of vintage cars and car-related events you’ve attended, creating separate topics for each set of visuals. This section provides a platform for enthusiasts to showcase their classic rides and document memorable experiences.

Is there a section for appreciating unrestored cars on the AACA Forum?

Absolutely! The Unrestored Car Appreciation Forum is dedicated to celebrating the beauty and historical significance of unrestored antique automobiles. Share photos, videos, and stories of original condition classic cars and events, creating separate topics for different sets of visuals.

How can I stay informed about automotive legislation on the AACA Forum?

In the Automotive Legislation section, members discuss and share information about laws, regulations, and policies that impact the ownership, restoration, and operation of vintage vehicles. This section provides a reliable source of information for enthusiasts navigating the legal aspects of classic car ownership.

Can I buy or sell antique automobiles and parts on the AACA Forum?

Yes! The Automobiles and Parts – Buy/Sell section is a marketplace for members to connect and trade antique automobiles and related parts and accessories. Additionally, there are marque-specific buy/sell forums dedicated to various classic car brands, including Chrysler, Ford, Buick, Pontiac, and more.

How can I learn how to use the AACA Forum effectively?

Visit the Forum How-To and Instructions section for valuable resources on navigating the forum. You can learn how to create topics, reply to discussions, navigate different sections, and utilize various features of the forum, ensuring you can easily find the information you need and engage in meaningful conversations.

What are the benefits of joining the AACA Forum and the Antique Automobile Club of America?

By becoming a member, you gain access to an extensive automotive library, national tours, an award-winning magazine, discussions covering thousands of topics, local regions and chapters, conventions, shows, events, and a monthly newsletter. AACA membership offers a wealth of resources, networking opportunities, and a sense of community for auto enthusiasts and collectors.
