NJPW Showdown Meet &amp; Greets Set For San Jose &amp; LA

New Japan Pro-Wrestling has announced more details regarding their upcoming meet and greet events set to occur during New Japan Showdown shows in San Jose and Los Angeles. According to the company, a handful of the biggest NJPW superstars will be on hand, although with two different days separating the meet and greets, not every superstar will be available on both days.

To see the lineup for meet and greet set for San Jose on November 9, check out below:

Kazuchika Okada
Will Ospreay
Jay White
SANADA

Below, you’ll find the meet and greet lineup for the second event, which takes place in Los Angeles on November 11:

Kota Ibushi
Toru Yano
Minoru Suzuki
SANADA

For more information about the events, make sure to head over to the NJPW website, where you’ll find ticket information.
