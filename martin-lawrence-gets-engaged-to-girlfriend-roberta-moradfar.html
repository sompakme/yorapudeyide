Martin Lawrence Gets Engaged To Girlfriend Roberta Moradfar

As we all wait patiently for Bad Boys 3 to make its way into theaters, some heartwarming news about our favorite comic hit the Internet. It was announced Friday, (March 31) Martin Lawrence popped the question to his girlfriend Roberta Moradfar, and of course, she said yes!

Moradfar, an aesthetics nurse practitioner, shared the news on her Instagram along with the gorgeous ring. There’s no word on whether or not this is Moradfar’s marriage, however, this will be Lawrence’s third. He was married to Patricia Southall from 1995-1996, and then was with Shamick Gibbs  from 2010 to 2012.

Congrats to the happy couple.
