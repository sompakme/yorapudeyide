Manny Khoshbin Biography; Net Worth, Age, Height, Company, House, Cars And Wife

Manny Khoshbin is an Iranian businessman, commercial real estate investor and author best known as the president and CEO of The Khoshbin Company.

Manuchehr “Manny” Khoshbin was born on 14th June 1971 in Iran. Not much is known about his Iranian parents but he migrated to the United States with his parents at the age of 14.

Manny Khoshbin has three siblings. As per his educational background, Manny went to West Miner high school where he graduated from in the year 1989.

Manny Khoshbin Height

Manny Khoshbin Company

Manny Khoshbin is currently the is the CEO and president of The Khoshbin Company which is a private commercial real estate company mainly based in Irvine, California.

Manny Khoshbin House
