In 2001, Johnson’s career took a significant turn when he joined Hendrick Motorsports, one of the most successful teams in NASCAR. This marked the beginning of a new chapter in his racing journey as he made his debut in the prestigious Winston Cup Series, now known as the NASCAR Cup Series.
