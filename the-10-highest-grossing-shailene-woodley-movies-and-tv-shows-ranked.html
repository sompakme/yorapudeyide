The 10 Highest-Grossing Shailene Woodley Movies And TV Shows, Ranked

Grab your popcorn and get ready for a cinematic journey through the remarkable career of Shailene Woodley! Whether you know her as a teenage drama queen or a fearless heroine, one thing's for sure; this talented actress has left her mark on the movie industry. In this article, we'll take you through her top 10 highest-grossing movies of all time. Yes, you read that right, we're talking worldwide box office gross here! From fighting for survival in a dystopian world to navigating complex relationships, Woodley has portrayed a diverse range of characters with grace and finesse. So, join us as we uncover what makes each of these movies so special, and why Woodley's star power continues to shine bright on the big screen.

The Spectacular Now - 2013 ($6.9 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this coming-of-age movie, Woodley plays the role of Aimee, a shy and reserved girl who falls for the charming but troubled Sutter. This indie gem grossed $6.9 million worldwide and showcased Woodley's talent for bringing depth and authenticity to her characters. Woodley's performance was praised by critics and audiences alike, cementing her reputation as a rising star in Hollywood.

Snowden - 2016 ($37 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Woodley plays the role of Lindsay Mills in this political thriller about Edward Snowden. This movie explores the life of a man who becomes a whistleblower, and the repercussions that follow. Despite mixed reviews, Snowden performed well at the box office, grossing $37 million worldwide. Woodley's portrayal of Mills added depth to the movie and made it worth watching. Her chemistry with co-star Joseph Gordon-Levitt was also a highlight of the film, adding a layer of romance to an otherwise intense story.

Adrift - 2018 ($59 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Based on a true story, Adrift tells the tale of a young couple who set out on a sailing trip only to encounter a catastrophic hurricane. Woodley plays the role of Tami Oldham, a woman who must fight for survival against all odds. This thrilling movie grossed $59 million worldwide and solidified Woodley's position as a true Hollywood star. Woodley's performance in this physically demanding role was praised by critics, demonstrating her range and versatility as an actress.

The Descendants - 2011 ($177 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this family drama, Woodley plays the role of Alexandra, the troubled daughter of a wealthy landowner in Hawaii. This critically acclaimed movie grossed $177 million worldwide and earned Woodley a Golden Globe nomination for her performance. Her portrayal of Alexandra was complex and nuanced, adding depth to an already powerful movie. Woodley's chemistry with co-star George Clooney was palpable, making their on-screen relationship one of the highlights of the movie.

Allegiant - 2016 ($179 Million)

Via Flickr/Tribun

In the third installment of the Divergent series, Tris and her friends journey beyond the walls of their society to discover the truth about their world. This movie grossed $179 million worldwide and was a thrilling conclusion to the series. Woodley's performance as Tris was powerful and poignant, making it a standout in the franchise. Despite mixed reviews, Woodley's talent and screen presence shone through, reminding audiences of her ability to anchor a blockbuster movie.

Divergent - 2014 ($288 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this dystopian thriller, Woodley plays the role of Tris Prior, a young woman who must navigate a society divided into factions. This blockbuster movie performed well at the box office, grossing $288 million worldwide. Woodley's portrayal of Tris was both fierce and vulnerable, making this movie a hit with audiences. The success of Divergent launched Woodley into the spotlight and solidified her status as a leading lady in Hollywood.

Insurgent - 2015 ($297 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In the second installment of the Divergent series, Woodley's character Tris is on the run from the faction leaders who seek to eliminate her. This action-packed movie grossed $297 million worldwide and showcased Woodley's ability to take on challenging roles and make them her own. Woodley's performance as Tris continued to impress audiences and critics, cementing her status as one of Hollywood's leading actresses.

The Fault in Our Stars - 2014 ($307 Million)

Via Flickr/Hobby

In this heartwarming movie, Woodley plays the role of Hazel, a teenage cancer patient who falls in love with Augustus Waters. This emotional rollercoaster performed incredibly well at the box office, grossing $307 million worldwide. Woodley's performance was a standout, making this movie a must-watch for anyone who loves a good love story. Her portrayal of Hazel was both heartbreaking and uplifting, and her chemistry with co-star Ansel Elgort was electric. This movie is a testament to Woodley's talent for bringing depth and emotion to her characters.

Big Little Lies (TV Series) - 2017-2019 ($380 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this hit TV series, which is the only TV Series that made this list, Woodley plays the role of Jane Chapman, a young mother who becomes entangled in a web of secrets and lies. This critically acclaimed show grossed $380 million worldwide and earned Woodley a Primetime Emmy nomination for her performance. Her portrayal of Jane was both vulnerable and strong, making her character one of the most memorable in the series.

The Amazing Spider-Man 2 - 2014 ($709 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In this superhero blockbuster, Woodley plays the role of Mary Jane Watson, a love interest of Peter Parker. Although her scenes were ultimately cut from the movie, Woodley's involvement in this high-grossing franchise helped to solidify her status as one of Hollywood's rising stars. The Amazing Spider-Man 2 grossed an impressive $709 million worldwide, making it Woodley's highest-grossing movie to date.
