Khloe Kardashian Opens Up About Divorce From Lamar Odom

Khloe Kardashian is finally opening up. In an interview with her mother/ momager, Kris, for the April issue of Cosmopolitan, she talks about trying to live a private life in the public eye and her high-profile split from Lamar Odom.

“I’m not a big ‘cry on a shoulder’ person. I’m very introverted when it comes to my own stuff. Everyone has issues, and I’m not someone who likes to burden people,” said the 29-year-old reality star. She also expressed her thoughts on possibly becoming a mother one day.

Last December, Khloe filed for divorce from the basketball star after their whirlwind romance came crashing down following rumors of his alleged drug addiction and infidelity rumors.

Despite the heartache, she says she “wouldn’t change a thing.”

She continued, “I don’t have any regrets…you learn as you go.”

“I love kids,” she says. “I hope and pray I have kids when the time is right.”

The new issue hits stands March 4.
