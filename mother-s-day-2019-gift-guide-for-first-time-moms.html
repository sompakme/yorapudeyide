Mothers Day 2019 GiftGuidefor First-Time Moms

There’s a first time for everything! And for many, Sunday, May 12, will be their first time celebrating Mother’s Day.

Let’s face it, new moms are in need — but that just makes life easy for their friends, partners and coworkers when it comes to picking the perfect present. Whether shoppers go for more practical items or products to pamper, the options are endless.

For example, having a baby means saying goodbye to pregnancy and hello to all the things moms were missing out on while they were expecting. Whether they’re craving alcohol or intense workouts, that’s where Pink Buddha Life’s floral yoga mats or Señor Sangria’s bottles can come in handy.

On the other hand, motherhood also results in massive lists of must-haves. When it comes to diaper bags, strollers and baby gates, it can be hard to narrow down the good from the bad without trial and error or recommendations. So pick up Alf the Label’s Stella Bag, Mockingbird’s Stroller or Fusion Gates’ interchangeable art screens to save a new parent some time and money.
