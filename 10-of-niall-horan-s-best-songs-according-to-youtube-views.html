10 Of Niall Horan's Best Songs (According To YouTube Views)

Highlights

  • "Slow Hands" has been viewed by over 240 million fans, making it Niall Horan's top-ranked song on YouTube, with fans loving the gritty sound and heavy bass.
  • "This Town" introduced Niall Horan as a solo artist, reaching 83 million views on YouTube with simple moving drawings in the video.
  • "Heartbreak Weather" album featured hits like "Nice To Meet Ya" and "Too Much To Ask," each with over 64 million views on YouTube.

Niall Horan is a former member of One Direction, but since the band split up, he's gone off on his own to create albums as a solo artist. He has been quite successful in this venture as his songs top the charts due to their catchy lyrics and his vocals. YouTube is one of the many platforms where fans listen to his music, and they have been heard millions of times.

This list seeks to rank his songs from his solo career according to how many times they have been viewed on YouTube. Some of the numbers might surprise fans as they discover just how many people are as obsessed with his music as they are.

Keep reading to learn the ranking of Niall Horan's best songs!

Updated April 2024:These are the most viewed Niall Horan songs on YouTube to date. The list will continue to be updated as new songs enter the list and as the videos receive more views.

10 Seeing Blind

7.2 Million Views

Album

Flicker

Release Date

June 1, 2018

This song debuted on Horan's album called Flicker and this acoustic version has had over 7.2 million views.

He sings the song with popular country artist, Maren Morris. As it turns out, this song turned into a pop-country single. This video features them in a warmly lit studio that plays into the vibe this song is trying to convey to its listeners.

9 Heartbreak Weather

17 Million Views

Album

Heartbreak Weather

Release Date

March 13, 2020

Horan throws it back in time as he puts on a turtleneck and tries out some retro dance movies for this music video.

It has been viewed over 17 million times on YouTube, and it is on an album by the same name. Fans everywhere can agree that Niall Horan would make an excellent weatherman after watching him in this video.

8 No Judgement

20 Million Views

Album

Heartbreak Weather

Release Date

February 7, 2020

The video for No Judgement has been seen over 20 million times on YouTube and is from his album called Heartbreak Weather.

Fans are obsessed with the smooth melody and catchy lyrics that everyone can sing along to.

The music video itself is a little weird as it follows the strange things an older couple does as they no longer care what their significant other thinks of their quirks.

7 Put A Little Love On Me

25 Million Views

Album

Heartbreak Weather

Release Date

December 6, 2019

The album called Heartbreak Weather also gave fans this song that is slow and soft in a way that brings out emotion in its listeners. It has been viewed over 25 million times on YouTube due to its lovely nature.

It is believed that this ballad was written in response to his breakup with Hailee Steinfeld back in 2018. The dancer in the music video even holds a resemblance to her as well, which only brought fans to tears as they felt the passion he put into the lyrics.

6 Flicker

43 Million Views

Album

Flicker

Release Date

October 20, 2017

This is the acoustic version of his song "Flicker", which was released on an album of the same name.

It is another soft and beautiful song like the previous one was, as it is another song about a person left without their loved one. The video has been viewed over 43 million times on YouTube.

The video pans around the room to the various musicians as Horan pours his heart and soul into the music he sings into the microphone.

5 On The Loose

51 Million Views

Album

Flicker

Release Date

February 16, 2018

Those looking for a more upbeat tempo from Horan should look no further than this song. It is another song from his album called Flicker and it has been viewed over 51 million times on YouTube.

It carries some Fleetwood Mac vibes in its sound which fans loved as many had grown up on their music. The music video itself is filmed in a desert-like area and features Horan as he goes after a woman who enjoys her freedom.

4 Too Much To Ask

64 Million Views

Album

Flicker

Release Date

September 15, 2017

The album called Flicker also gave the world this song that has been viewed over 64 million times on YouTube.

It is heartfelt but focuses on a simpler tune that focuses on his lyrics rather than a complicated background beat. The music video contributes to this downtrodden vibe as he is seen alone as he struggles to move past a breakup.

3 Nice To Meet Ya

76 Million Views

Album

Heartbreak Weather

Release Date

October 4, 2019

This song was on his album called Heartbreak Weather and it has been seen by fans over 76 million times.

Fans love the catchy beat of the music as it has everyone up on their feet. The music video itself tells a story as it follows Horan and his interaction with a woman on the street as he eventually ends up with her number.

2 This Town

83 Million Views

Album

Flicker

Release Date

September 29, 2016

This song is what put Horan in the spotlight when he first debuted as a solo artist with his first album called Flicker. It has been viewed over 83 million times on YouTube and the video is a bit different as it features a variety of simple moving drawings.

The song helped separate him from his previous music with his bandmates as he combined passionate lyrics with a folk-type sound.

1 Slow Hands

240 Million Views

Album

Flicker

Release Date

May 4, 2017

The song at the top of this ranking is none other than Slow Hands, which was released on his album called Flicker.

It features a heavy bass with a gritty sound that fans instantly fell in love with. It charted well in numerous countries as fans from all over the world brought it up to 240 million views on YouTube.
