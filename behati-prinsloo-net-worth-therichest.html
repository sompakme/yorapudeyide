Behati Prinsloo

Birthname Behati Prinsloo Birthdate May 16, 1989 Birthplace Grootfontein, Namibia Height 5' 10" (1.78 m) Profession Model Net worth $3,000,000 Source of Wealth Modeling Nationality Namibia Marital Status In a relationship (Adam Levine)

Namibian model Behati Prinsloo has an estimated net worth of $3 million in 2013. She is best recognized as the face of Victoria's Secret sub-brand Pink. A year later, she became a Victoria's Secret Angel.

From 2007 through 2o12, Prinsloo has appeared in the fashion shows of Victoria's Secret. She ranked 14th on model.com's list of 'The Top Sexiest Models'. In addition to Victoria's Secret, she has also walked on the fashion shows for Miu Miu, Prada and Chanel. She was also featured in the ad campaigns for DKNY Jeans, Nine West, Nina Ricci, Gap, Tommy Hilfiger, Marc by Marc Jacobs, Chanel, Max Studio, Adore, Kurt Geiger, Aquascutum, Hugo Boss, and H&M. Furthermore, Prinsloo was featured on the covers of The Daily Telegraph magazine, French, Swedish & Italian Elle, American Velvet, Russian Vogue, Vogue and Italy's Muse.

Behati Prinsloo was born on May 16, 1989 in Grootfontein, South-West Africa.
