Gisele Bundchen Gets Teary Talking About Her Divorce From Tom Brady

A divorce is always a difficult thing to get over, and fans shouldn't forget that just because the people involved are famous. Gisele Bundchen and Tom Brady announced their divorce in late 2022, and although they have both been slowly moving on, it's understandably still a touchy subject.

Recently, in a sneak peek of a candid interview the supermodel gave, the audience got to see a rare vulnerable moment where Gisele broke down in tears talking about the end of her marriage.

In conversation with interviewer Robin Roberts, the two women spoke about how the end of a relationship is always surprising, as no one walks into a marriage expecting it to end. When asked the simple question "how are you?", Gisele Bundchen couldn't pretend and got teary, asking the camera crew for a moment to compose herself.

The model had previously compared the divorce to the "death of a dream," and although it still feels raw and sad, in this new chapter of her life, she's grown and learned a lot.

"Everything I've experienced, it made me realize what I want, and what I don't want," Gisele shared. "At the end of the day, if you're not truthful to who you are, then it doesn't work."

She was also asked about her co-parenting relationship with Tom Brady, and her ambiguous answer surprised a lot of people. "I think there's easier days than others," she said. "I can only control what I do."

Gisele Avoided Questions About A New Romance

While processing the end of her marriage of over a decade, taking care of her children, and advancing in her career, it seems Gisele Bundchen has also been moving on, although she appeared reluctant to speak publicly about it.

She has recently been linked to Joaquim Valente, a Brazilian jiu-jitsu instructor who she's reportedly been dating for several months, but when she was asked about whether she was ready to "open up her heart" to someone new, Gisele played coy and looked away.
