Matt Hardy (And Matt Hardy) Announced As AEW Fight Forever Pre-Order Bonus

Matt Hardy is ready to Fight Forever.

AEW Fight Forever will feature two playable versions of Matt Hardy as a pre-order bonus. Fans who pre-order the game will unlock ‘Broken’ Matt, as well as the regular Matt Hardy vessel.

Hardy announced the news on social media, sharing the following announcement video:

The latest episode of All Elite Arcade revealed new wrestlers and showed off a lot of gameplay of AEW Fight Forever.

Matt and Nick Jackson of the Young Bucks, and Eddie Kingston’s intros were showcased during the stream, as well as six different matches. In addition, more announcements were teased, including an “Elite Edition” of the game. Evil Uno also said the full roster reveal for the game would be announced very soon.

Read a full recap of the latest stream here.

The AEW Fight Forever: Elite Edition was officially announced on Thursday morning. Full details about the content included in this version of the game are available here.

AEW Fight Forever will release on Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, and PC on Thursday, June 29.
