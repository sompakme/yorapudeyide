Heres Why Fans Are Irate With The 'Big Brother 22' Production Team

Finally, we’re starting to see some fireworks during the 'Big Brother All-Stars' season.

With both Da’Vonne and Bayleigh on the block, rest assured, both contestants won’t go out quietly. It’ll make for an explosive eviction night, that we can guarantee.

Production Is Rubbing Fans The Wrong Way

Like every season, there is some controversy taking place in the house and fans are none too pleased with the way production seems to be handling it. The recent episode was all about Tyler, potentially putting himself on the block for Da’Vonne and Bayleigh, though ultimately, that was a no-go, with Christmas and his alliance turning down the notion.

The fans felt as though it was just a way for production to stir the pot and add a little bit more drama. In truth, fans weren’t pleased that they didn’t shed enough of a light on another touchy matter, Ian’s struggle with autism.

A lot of his struggle was barely shown on the episode, instead, we just got a quick run-down that he was set to miss the competition. What the show didn’t show its viewers was the backlash that apparently took place among the houseguests, it is said that some would even go as far as to mock Ian. We’ve seen such controversy take place in the past – bullying has been a major problem on the show throughout the last few seasons.

We know Julie Chen isn’t afraid to put the contestants on blast, it’ll be interesting to see if she decides to bring the issue up in the upcoming eviction night, or if production revisits the scenario due to all the backlash online, especially among several former contestants.

Adding insult, fan theories suggest that Tyler was trying to gather sympathy votes with the Black Lives Matter movement, siding with Da’Vonne and Bayleigh.

Clearly, the episode had everyone talking and not in the most positive way, especially considering that later in the episode, Tyler would basically go back on everything that he said, given that word spread on Bayleigh still being furious with Tyler, despite the game-saving mood.

Expect some drama to take place in the next episode.
