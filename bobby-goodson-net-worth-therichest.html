Bobby Goodson

Birthname Bobby Goodson Birthplace Jacksonville, North Carolina Education Chowan College Profession Businessman Net worth $3,000,000 Source of Wealth Television, Goodson's All Terrain Logging Nationality United States Country United States Marital Status Married (Lori Mueller) Children 2 (Justin, Britney)

American TV personality and entrepreneur Bobby Goodson has an estimated net worth of $3 million in 2012.

Bobby Goodson was born in 1964 in Jacksonville, North Carolina. At age 11, he started working in the logging industry. At age 13, he met his future wife, Lori Mueller. A year later, the two started dating and since then, they have been together. The couple has two children together, Justin and Britney. He went to Chowan College where he took a degree in Graphics Communication.

Currently, he runs and manages his Goodson's All Terrain Logging which headquarters in Pender County, North Carolina. It was in 1986 when he started his first business. Today, he owns his own slogging trucks, and currently employs 17 people together with Justin.

In addition to being a businessman, Bobby Goodson is also a reality TV personality. He appears on "Swamp Loggers" which features the company as well as the everyday struggle of Goodson and his 17 employees in keeping the business going. Aired on Discovery Channel, Swamp Loggers is now in its fourth season.
