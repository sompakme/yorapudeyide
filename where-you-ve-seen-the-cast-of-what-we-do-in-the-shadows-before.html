Where You've Seen The Cast Of What We Do In The Shadows Before

The hit FXX series What We Do In The Shadows is the continuation of the popular mockumentary film that follows the adventures of a house of vampires as they navigate life in the 21st century. The film and show pay perfect homage to the Vampire sub-genre of horror while also being playful and hilarious. So funny is the show that the audience often forgets that it's about creatures who survive by sucking the blood of humans.

Related: The 'What We Do In The Shadows' Cast Member With The Highest Net Worth

The film version starred Jemaine Clement of Flight of the Conchords fame and Taika Waititi, who is now one of the most in-demand directors in Hollywood. The pair are attached as executive producers to the show, and they have reprised their popular roles from the film. But what about the rest of the cast, where have we seen them before?

15 Natasia Demetriou

The actress who plays Nadia can be found in several films and shows from England. What We Do In The Shadows is arguably her breakout role for American audiences. But fans of British comedy can find her in Stath Lets Flats, Ellie and Natasia, and The Big Flower Fight. She was also in Will Ferrell's 2020 project, Eurovision.

14 Harvey Guillen

The Mexican-American actor who plays Guillermo the vampire's familiar has been in films like Status Update, Truth or Dare, and Eye Candy. He is also signed on for 2023's Blue Beetle from Marvel.

13 Kristen Schaal

Schaal should not need any introduction as her distinct voice lets everyone know she is Louise Belcher on Bob's Burgers. This is not her first time doing a show involving Jemaine Clement - she played Mel in his breakthrough show Flight of the Conchords.

Related: 10 Stars Who Have Voiced Characters On 'Bob's Burgers'

12 Jake McDorman

McDorman, AKA Jeff Suckler, can be found in numerous films and TV shows ranging from the TV version of Limitless to Bradley Cooper's American Sniper. In 2022, he had a role in Larry and Marge Go Large starring Bryan Cranston and Annette Bening.

11 Veronika Slowikowska

Slowikowska has only recently broken out as an actress, though she has a handful of unrecognizable roles to her name. Audiences will most likely recognize her from the show Homeschooled which she joined in 2022.

10 Anthony Atamanuik

Audiences will most likely recognize him for his impression of Donald Trump for Comedy Central's The President Show. He is also a successful improv comic and screenwriter.

9 Chris Sandiford

Sandiford is part of a popular comedy duo called Ladies and Gentlemen. Films and shows to his name include God's Favorite Idiot, Zoe, Fear the Walking Dead, and Cavendish.

8 Arj Barker

Like Kristen Schaal, Arj Barker also worked with Jemaine Clement on Flight of the Conchords. He is also a popular stand-up comedian with several specials and albums to his name. He also sparked up with stoner comedian Doug Benson for his pot documentary Super High Me.

7 Anoop Desai

American Idol fans already know that Desai was a contestant in the eighth season. He graduated into a healthy music career, and eventually became an actor thanks to roles in shows like Billions and Russian Doll.

6 Parisa Fakhri

Fakhri is both a working actress and an accomplished voice-over artist. She can be seen, or heard, in Agents of Shield, Hollywood Heights, The Thundermans, and Dreamland.

5 Doug Jones

Doug Jones is famously not famous because he is usually the actor Hollywood casts for roles requiring heavy makeup. He has played creatures in several films and shows like The Shape of Water, Pan's Labyrinth, Hell Boy, and Hocus Pocus (he was the zombie the witches brought back to life).

4 Beanie Feldstein

Feldstein has been in everything from shows like American Crime Story to Oscar-nominated films like Ladybird. Until 2022, she was playing Fanny in a Broadway reboot of Funny Girl. Although she uses a different stage name, she is the only sibling of actor Jonah Hill.

Related: What Happened To Beanie Feldstein's Run On Broadway

3 Mark Proksch

Fans of The Office will immediately recognize Mark Proksch as Nate, the momma's boy who works in the warehouse and often serves as Dwight's lackey. He is also a close collaborator with Adult Swim comedian Tim Heidecker and can be found in almost every one of Heidecker's projects. He was also Daniel, the baseball card-collecting novice of a drug dealer in Better Call Saul, fans of that show might remember him better as the poor guy who had to make a video of him "squat cobbling" (Google it.)

2 Kayan Novak

Arguably, Nandor the Relentless is one of the most popular characters on the show. He is played by Iranian actor Kayan Novak. Novak was in Disney's 2021 film Cruella and in the 2014 film Cuban Fury. Before What We Do In The Shadows, he did shows like Fonejacker and Facejacker.

1 Matt Berry

Berry has become one of the most popular institutions of the show thanks to his numerous quotable lines, many of which have become viral TikTok sounds, like "You really are the most devious bastard, in, New York City..." Berry was in Toast of London, The I.T. Crowd, and The Mighty Boosh. He was also a professor in an episode of Dan Harmon's Community. He can also be seen in the Star Wars series The Book of Boba Fett.
