Santino Marella, Ultimate X, More Set For IMPACT Rebellion 2023

Following the April 6 episode of IMPACT on AXS, several new matches are set for IMPACT Rebellion 2023.

Including the in-ring return of Santino Marella to pay-per-view, an Ultimate X bout for the IMPACT World Tag Titles, and a Triple Threat Elimination Match for the X-Division Title, four new matches were added to IMPACT Rebellion.

Here’s the full card for IMPACT Rebellion so far:

  • – KUSHIDA vs. Steve Maclin
  • – Jordynne Grace vs. Deonna Purrazzo
  • – Bullet Club (Ace Austin & Chris Bey) (c) vs. The Motor City Machine Guns (Alex Shelley & Chris Sabin)
  • – Trey Miguel (c) vs. “Speedball” Mike Bailey vs. Jonathan Gresham
  • – The Coven (KiLynn King & Taylor Wilde) (c) vs. The Death Dollz (Rosemary & Jessicka)
  • – Team Bully (Bully Ray, Kenny King, Masha Slamovich, John Skylar & Jason Hotch) vs. Team Dreamer (Tommy Dreamer, Frankie Kazarian, Yuya Uemura, Killer Kelly & TBA)
  • Santino Marella, Joe Hendry, Dirty Dango vs. The Design
