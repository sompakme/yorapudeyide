Clayton Kershaw is a professional baseball pitcher who plays for the Los Angeles Dodgers of Major League Baseball.

Clayton Kershaw height

How tall is Clayton Kershaw? What is Clayton Kershaw’s height? Clayton Kershaw is 1.93 meters tall, which is 193 centimeters or 6 feet and 3 inches tall.

Clayton Kershaw age

How old is Clayton Kershaw? What is Clayton Kershaw’s age? Clayton Kershaw is 34 years old; born on March 19, 1988.

Clayton Kershaw contract

Clayton Kershaw salary

How much does Clayton Kershaw receive as a salary? How much is Clayton Kershaw’s salary? Clayton Kershaw receives an average annual salary of $31 million.

Clayton Kershaw college
