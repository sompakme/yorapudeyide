Real Madrid, a globally recognized and respected football club, holds an impressive track record of wins.

The team has secured an incredible 13 Champions League titles and 34 La Liga trophies, marking their dominance in the sport. Their exemplary performance across multiple leagues has garnered them international recognition.

Their prestigious status extends to the compensation with which they provide their players. Many of these individuals rank among football's highest earners, raking in millions of euros every week thanks to lucrative contracts offered by the Spanish titans.

So who are the richest members on Real Madrid's roster as we head into 2024?

Let's do a rundown of the biggest income earners currently on the squad – tallied according to verified weekly earning reports.

8 Thibaut Courtois

€346,154 Per Week

Internationally praised Belgian goalie Thibaut Courtois is globally regarded as a premier player in his role, consistently making essential blocks and commanding his area with assured certainty.

His career took an influential turn in 2018 when he moved from Chelsea to Real Madrid for a notable €35 million agreement.

Upon joining the club, Courtois confirmed his standing as the indisputable primary goalkeeper for both his team and the nation.

His outstanding performances helped Real Madrid secure two La Liga championships, which highlights his crucial role within the team.

7 Federico Valverde

€360,000 Per Week

The player from Uruguay who takes the position of midfield for Real Madrid is recognized as one of their most elastic and adaptable members, experienced in varied roles like box-to-box, protective, or offensive midfielder.

His accomplishments include bagging two Champions League titles and dual La Liga trophies while serving under Real Madrid.

6 Antonio Rüdiger

€360,000 Per Week

The dynamic defender hailing from Germany became a part of Real Madrid after leaving Chelsea in 2022, committing to a term that will last four years at a cool €6.8 million per season.

Rüdiger is appreciated for his robustness and trustworthiness as a center-back, while also possessing skills necessary to perform as either a right-back or a guarded midfielder.

Establishing an unshakable partnership with David Alaba has resulted in claiming victory over 15 clean sheets out of 25 games they played together for Real Madrid.

Additionally, he is the proud holder of a Champions League title, winning the competition with Chelsea in 2021 prior to his move to the Spanish team.

4 Jude Bellingham

€400,577 Per Week

Hailing from England and endowed with extraordinary talent is Jude Bellingham, who recently became an integral addition to Real Madrid's powerful midfield.

His transfer from Germany's Borussia Dortmund in 2023 set another record as it was valued at an astounding figure of €103 million - marking it as the club’s second-priciest acquisition to date.

At Real Madrid, Bellingham found immediate success, fitting right into their gameplay and becoming a key piece within their strategic puzzle on-field.

He stands out for his commanding speed, robust strength, and superior ball-handling ability – coupled with gameplay insights that reflect wisdom beyond his youthful age.

Barely making ten appearances for the team so far in the whites of Madrid has provided a preview into his immense value through four goals already under his belt, also setting up six more for others to score via assists.

This quick settling down period combined with outstanding contributions strongly affirms him as one of today’s most exciting talents in international football.
