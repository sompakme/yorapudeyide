Brandin Tawan Cooks is an American football wide receiver for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League. He played college football at Oregon State, where he received consensus All-American honors, and was selected by the New Orleans Saints in the first round of the 2014 NFL Draft.

Brandin Cooks Wife: Meet Briannon Lepman
