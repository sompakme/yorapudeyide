Stephanie Mead WISH-TV, Channel 8 Weather and Wikipedia

Stephanie joined the Storm Track 8 team in April of 2017. She comes to WISH-TV from WGBA in Green Bay, Wisconsin. There, Mead forecasts sub-zero temperatures for the Green Bay Packers often on the frozen tundra of Lambeau Field.

Steph also covered numerous severe weather events in Northeast Wisconsin, including a blizzard as well as a dangerous thunderstorm, that delayed the PGA Championship at Whistling Straits.

Steph was an intern with WTVO-TV Channel 17 for 3 months. She then began her career at the Northern Television Center at DeKalb as a weather presenter and reporter for 2 years since 2011. She juggled her work and studies as an undergraduate.

She joined KMVT-TV in Twin Falls, Idaho after she graduated in 2013 doing the weather and also as a reporter. She was working under chief meteorologist Brian Neudorff and took over to present the weather on weekend nights.

In April 2015, Mead joined NBC 26 at Green Bay, Wisconsin delivering weather forecasts and feature reports to the newsroom. She made the move in order to be closer to her family in the Midwest.

She remained there for 2 years, forecasting in sub-zero temperatures from Lambeau Field for the Green Bay Packers and reporting on severe weather conditions such as blizzards and thunderstorms.
