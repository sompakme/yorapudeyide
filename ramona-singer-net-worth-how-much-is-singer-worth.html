Early Life and Career

Ramona Singer, born on November 17, 1956, in Southampton, New York, has had a fascinating journey to success. From a young age, Singer had a deep passion for fashion, which led her to pursue a degree in Marketing from the Fashion Institute of Technology. After graduating, she started her career as a buyer for Macy’s, where she gained valuable experience in the industry.

Her expertise eventually led her to work with renowned fashion brands such as The French Connection and Calvin Klein in the wholesale clothing business. Singer’s keen eye for trends and business acumen helped her excel in her role and build a strong reputation in the fashion world.

Not content with just working in the fashion industry, Singer decided to take her entrepreneurial spirit to the next level. She launched her own anti-aging skincare line called Ageless by Ramona, which further solidified her position as a trusted figure in the beauty and wellness space. Her dedication and hard work paid off when she was given the opportunity to join the cast of “Real Housewives of New York” in 2008.

“Fashion is my passion, and I’ve always been drawn to the entrepreneurial side of the industry. I wanted to create products that would empower women and make them feel confident and beautiful. Joining the cast of ‘Real Housewives of New York’ was a dream come true, and it has opened up so many doors for me in terms of career opportunities and personal growth.”

Since then, Singer has become a household name, captivating audiences with her vibrant personality and unique sense of style. Her career earnings have multiplied as she continues to balance her role on the reality show with her business ventures. The combination of her early experiences in the fashion industry, her entrepreneurial ventures, and her appearances on “Real Housewives of New York” has contributed significantly to her impressive net worth.
