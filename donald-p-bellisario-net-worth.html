Donald P. Bellisario Net Worth

Donald P. Bellisario

Birthname Donald Paul Bellisario Birthdate August 8, 1935 Birthplace Cokeburg, Pennsylvania Height 6' 2" (1.88 m) Education Pennsylvania State University Profession Producer Net worth $250,000,000 Source of Wealth Television Nationality United States Country United States Marital Status Married (four times) Children seven children

American television producer and screenwriter Donald P. Bellisario has an estimated net worth of $250 million. Born Donald Paul Bellisario on August 8, 1935 in Cokeburg, Pennsylvania, USA, he served in the U.S. Marine Corps between 1955 and 1959 and attained the rank of Sergeant with the Marine Corps Good Conduct Medal award.

Bellisario graduated with a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Pennsylvania State University in 1961. He was named a Distinguished Alumnus in 2001. After graduation, he worked as an advertising copywriter in Lancaster, Pennsylvania until 1965. Three years later, he became creative director of the Bloom Agency in Dallas. He became the senior vice president after eight years then he moved to Hollywood to screenwriting and production.

Donald P. Bellisario is famous for creating successful and lucrative television shows such as NCIS (2003 to present), JAG (10 seasons), Magnum PI (eight seasons), Airwolf and Quantum Leap (four seasons).

Donald received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame on March 2, 2004. He heads the Los Angeles-based production company Belisarius Productions. He has been married four times and has seven children.
