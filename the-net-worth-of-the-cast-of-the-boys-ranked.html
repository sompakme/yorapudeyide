The latest installment of the American superhero television series The Boys just dropped last June 3, 2022. It might have felt like forever after the last season ended which was released on 2020. The previous was just an explosive outing as the metahuman antagonist burst with anger issues. There are a lot of things that needed answers right away but the series took 2 years to release its follow up season.

The American series which was developed by Eric Kripke is currently streaming on Amazon Prime Video. It is based on the comic book with the same name which was written by Garth Ennis and Darick Robertso. It was originally published through DC Comics before eventually moving to Dynamite Entertainment. Take a look at the characters of the American superhero television series The Boys and the net worth of the cast members.

Nick Wechsler

Nick Wechsler plays as Blue Hawk in the series. Blue Hawk also did not come from the original comics where the show was based from. He will be part of the latest season and fans will be delighted to find out more about his character in the show. American actor Nick Wechsler was born on September 3, 1978 in Albuquerque. He is best known for his roles in the show Roswell and Revenge. He currently has a net worth of $1.4 million.

Erin Elair Moriarty

Erin Elair Moriarty plays as Annie January / Starlight in the series. Starlight is a member of the group of superheroes funded by Vought Industries called Seven. She is among the characters which are based original comic books where the show was based from. American actress Erin Elair Moriarty was born on June 24, 1994 in New York, United States. She is best known for her roles in HBO's True Detective, ABC's Red Widow and Netflix's Jessica Jones. The 27 years old actress currently has a net worth of $1.5 million.

Miles Gaston Villanueva

Miles Gaston Villanueva plays the role of Supersonic. Supersonic is a new character and was not original part of the comic book where the show was based from. Supersonic is the ex boyfriend of Starlight played by Erin Moriarty. Actor and producer Miles Gaston Villanueva was born on March 14, 1989 in California. The 33 years old actor is best known for his roles in Nancy Drew and Law and Order. He has a net worth of $1.5 million.

Katia Winter

Katia Winter plays as Little Nina in the series. Little Nina is a character from the comic books where the show was based from. She is the Russian mob boss who was featured in some of the shocking moments from the book. Swedish actress Katia Winter was born on October 13, 1983 in Stockholm, Sweden. The 38 years old actress has been known for playing the role of Katrina Crane on the TV series titled Sleepy Hollow.

Sean Patrick Flanery

Sean Patrick Flanery plays the role of Gunpowder in the series. Gunpowder is one of the minor antagonists from the original comics where the show was based from. He is a firearms enthusiast who is a retired superhero. He is also part of the corrupt team called Teenage Kix. American actor and author Sean Patrick Flanery was born on October 11, 1965 in Lake Charles, Louisiana. The 56 years old actor is best known for his role in The Boondock Saints playing as Connor MacManus. He currently has a net worth of $3 million.

Laurie Holden

Laurie Holden plays as Crimson Countless in the series. Her character is sort of the parody of Scarlet Witch in the Marvel Comics. Crimson Countless is a member of the payback and is among the minor antagonist in the series. Canadian-American actress Laurie Holden was born on December 17, 1969 in Los Angeles, California. The 52 year old actress is best known for her roles in The Majestic, The Walking Dead series and The X-Files. She currently has a net worth of $3 million.

Kristin Booth

Kristin Booth plays the character of Tessa in the series. She is among the two TNT Twins. Canadian actress Kristin Booth was born on August 28, 1974 in Kitchener, Canada. The 47 years old actress is best known for her role in Signed, Sealed, Delivered franchise as Shane McInerney. She is an accomplished student when she was still studying and earned Honours BFA when she graduated from the Ryerson Theatre School at Ryerson University. She currently has a net worth of $5 million.

Karl Urban

Karl-Heinz Urban plays as Billy Butcher in the series. Billy Butcher is the amoral Super-hunter character on The Boys. New Zealand actor Karl-Heinz Urban was born on June 7, 1972 in Wellington, New Zealand. The 49 years old actor is best known for his role in TV series Xena: Warrior Princess. His first ever Hollywood role was starring in the horror film Ghost Ship in 2002. The New Zealander has currently has a net worth of $20 million.
