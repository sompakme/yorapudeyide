Whos In HBOs White Lotus Season 2 And What Are They Worth?

White Lotus is coming back for a second season with many new stars to join the cast as guests at the resort. Production likely received a bigger budget from the HBO network because they went all out to get many households name stars such as Tom Hollander and Theo James. Fans are excited for the new season to drop and are curious to know some new cast members joining the second season. So today, we are breaking down all the new characters and what their current net worth is (except for one returning cast member).

12 Adam DiMarco Is Worth $1 Million

First up on the list is Adam DiMarco who will play the role of Albie Di Grasso in season 2 of White Lotus. Adam has gained recognition over the past few years. The Canadian native also is beginning to gain many followers on his social media platforms. Adam is known for his roles in the Disney Channel movies Zapped and Radio Rebel. Lately, Adam has been having a recurring role in the Drama television series The Order.

11 Haley Lu Richardson Is Worth $1 Million

Another new face to star in season two of White Lotus will be the character Portia played by Haley Lu Richardson. Haley has become a rising star these days from her roles in the box office hit films Unpregnant and Five Feet Apart. It has been speculated by Deadline that Haley’s character Portia will be the sidekick of Jennifer Coolidge’s character Tanya.

10 Beatrice Grannó Is Worth $1.5 Million

Beatrice Granno is an Italian actress who may not be a household name quite yet. However, that will change soon enough as she is set to star in season 2 of White Lotus as Mia. Some fans may know Beatrice from her role as Maria Spezi in the 2021 film Security.

9 Sabrina Impacciatore Is Worth $1.5 Million

Sabrina Impacciatore will be playing the role of resort manager Valentina on White Lotus. Stephanie will surely have a busy schedule ahead of her. She is also set to star in Paul Ortiz’s upcoming film Across the River and Into the Trees.

8 Meghann Fahy Is Worth $1.5 Million

Meghann Fahy is set to star as Daphne Babcock in the new season of White Lotus. She will be the wife to Cameron Babcock who is on a double date vacation with Ethan and Harper Spiller. Meghann Fahy is most notable for her lead role of Sutton Brady from the comedy-drama television series The Bold Type. We are sure many fans of Meghann will be happy to see her return to the television screens in White Lotus.

7 Simona Tabasco Is Worth $2.14 Million

Simon Tabasco is set to play the role of Lucia when season two of White Lotus finally comes out. Simona is a well-known Italian actress who starred in 6 episodes of Luna Park as Nora Marini.

6 Will Sharpe Is Worth $3 Million

Will Sharpe may not have starred in many films and television series, but that will all change soon enough. Will is set to play the role of Ethan Spiller, a resort guest on a trip with his wife Harper. Will Sharpe is mostly known for his role on Sherlock in the role Corporal Lyons.
