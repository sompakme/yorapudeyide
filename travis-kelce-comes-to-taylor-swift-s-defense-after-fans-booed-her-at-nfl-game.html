Travis Kelce Comes To Taylor Swifts Defense After Fans Booed Her AT NFL Game

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift receives a negative response from some fans at a football game supporting her boyfriend, Travis Kelce. She remains unfazed and continues to cheer loudly for him.
  • Travis Kelce and his brother, Jason, comment on the crowd's reaction and emphasize how impressed they were with Taylor's popularity. They focus on the positive response from fans rather than the negativity.
  • Despite concerns from their inner circle about the fast-paced relationship, Taylor and Travis are still going strong and showing support for each other at various events.

Taylor Swift has been showing her boyfriend Travis Kelce public support by appearing at many of his football games in the last few months. But it hasn’t always been easy for the singer, as she was welcomed to a booing crowd at Kelce’s Chiefs game against the New England Patriots.

When Taylor arrived at Gillette Stadium in Foxborough, Mass., on Sunday, December 17, she was decked out in red and white – the signature colors for Travis’ team. But upon seeing her famous face, some Patriots fans began booing.

Taylor didn’t seem bothered by the negative response, as she proceeded to watch the entire game and shout loudly for her athlete boyfriend. She also hasn’t publicly addressed the boos.

Travis Says Taylor Wasn’t Bothered By The Haters

But Travis and his brother Jason Kelce directly commented on the crowd’s recent reaction to Taylor during the December 20th episode of their podcast New Heights – the same podcast where Travis mentioned his interest in Taylor, only for them to start dating after she caught wind.

“They showed Taylor at the game,” Travis told his older brother, emphasizing that the other team’s fans were clearly intimidated by Taylor’s presence. “You don’t see an entire home team fan base go insane for someone wearing the opposite colors."

“Just shows you how amazing that girl is,” the football star went on. “They went absolutely insane when they showed Taylor on the screen.”

Travis explained that while Taylor was aware some people were booing for her, she was more focused on the many audience members who were cheering in excitement over her appearance.
