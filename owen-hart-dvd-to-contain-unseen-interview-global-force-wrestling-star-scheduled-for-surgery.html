Owen Hart DVD To Contain Unseen Interview, Global Force Wrestling Star Scheduled For Surgery

Andrew Everett

Global Force Wrestling / Impact Wrestling roster member Andrew Everett recently announced he got MRI results back and will be undergoing surgery to repair a torn meniscus. 

Everett suffered the injury at the end of last month while working a show in England, and initially thought it was a reaggravation of a previous ACL injury. Everett noted that the procedure is a short surgery and he will be “back in no time.” 

Owen Hart

WrestlingDVDNetwork.com is reporting the upcoming Owen Hart DVD Owen: Hart Of Gold will feature a previously unseen interview with Owen.
