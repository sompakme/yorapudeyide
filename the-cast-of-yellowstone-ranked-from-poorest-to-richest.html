The Cast Of Yellowstone, Ranked From Poorest To Richest

Highlights

  • Yellowstone has become a highly successful show with a devoted fan base due to its captivating storyline and talented cast, including Oscar and Emmy winners.
  • Luke Grimes and Forrie J. Smith are among the wealthiest cast members of Yellowstone, with net worths ranging from $500,000 to $6 million.
  • Kevin Costner, who portrays the patriarchal leader in the show, has had a long and successful career, earning him a net worth of $250 million.

Even though Taylor Sheridan and John Linson have both created masterpieces in the past, it is highly unlikely they could predict that Yellowstone would cause this many waves. Ever since its premiere in 2018, the show has stayed at the top, keeping fans glued with the right amount of mischief, drama, and cliffhangers.

The show follows the life of crime of a patriarchal leader who owns the largest contiguous ranch in the United States. In addition to the series doing exceptionally well, some of its cast members are also Oscar and Emmy winners, and as expected, they are probably paid well and have made loads of money from their careers.
