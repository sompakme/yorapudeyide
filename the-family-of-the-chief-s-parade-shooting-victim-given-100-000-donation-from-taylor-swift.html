The Family Of The Chief's Parade Shooting Victim Given $100,000 Donation From Taylor Swift

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift donated $100,000 to family of Super Bowl shooting victim, expressing her sympathies for their loss.
  • Children made up 9 of the victims in the Kansas City Chiefs' Super Bowl celebration shooting.
  • Swift has become associated with the Kansas City Chiefs through her relationship with Travis Kelce, and regularly attends their games.

Celebration turned to chaos during Wednesday's Kansas City Chiefs Super Bowl celebration when gunshots were fired into the crowd, injuring 21 people and killing one, Elizabeth Lopez-Galvin. Variety's Chris Willman confirmed that Taylor Swift donated $100,000 to the family of the victim, with a note that read:

"Sending my deepest sympathies and condolences in the wake of your devastating loss."

Children in Missouri were given the day off from school so that they could celebrate the team's victory. Sadly, children made up 9 of the victims of the shooting.
