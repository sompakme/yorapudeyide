Couples Who Made Their Red Carpet Debuts at an Awards Show

Celebrities know the significance of going public with their relationships, especially when they do so in a high-profile way.

Justin Hartley and Sofia Pernas, for example, made a splash at the 2021 MTV Movie & TV Awards when they debuted matching rings one year after they were first linked in May 2020.

Us Weekly confirmed one day after the awards show that the This Is Us star and the soap opera actress had secretly tied the knot. The ceremony was “super intimate” and took place “very, very recently,” a source exclusively told Us in May 2021. Hartley’s teenage daughter, Isabella, was there to witness the romantic moment, per the insider.

Hartley’s surprise nuptials marked his third marriage. Hartley was previously married to his Passions costar Lindsay Korman for eight years before splitting in May 2012. He tied the knot for a second time with Chrishell Stause in October 2017 but ultimately called it quits with the Selling Sunset star in November 2019.
