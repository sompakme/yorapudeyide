New Title Matches Announced, Updated AEW Grand Slam 2023 Card

A few more matches have been confirmed for next week’s batch of AEW Grand Slam-themed episodes, and now we have a better look at the updated card for the event.

During tonight’s episode of AEW Collision, the company confirmed most of the matches set for next week, including the highly anticipated AEW World Championship Match between MJF and Samoa Joe, the Title vs. Title match between Claudio Castagnoli and Eddie Kingston, and more.

The fully updated card for both nights of AEW Grand Slam are below:

Night 1, September 20

  • : MJF (c) vs. Samoa Joe
  • : Saraya (c) vs. Toni Storm
  • Chris Jericho vs. Sammy Guevara
  • : Claudio Castagnoli (ROH World Championship) vs. Eddie Kingston (STRONG Openweight Championship)
  • : Jon Moxley (c) vs. Rey Fenix

Night 2, September 22

  • Christian Cage and Luchasaurus vs. Darby Allin & Sting
  • : Mogul Embassy (Brian Cage, Bishop Kaun, and Toa Liona) (c) (with Prince Nana) vs. The Elite (Matt Jackson, Nick Jackson, and “Hangman” Adam Page)
