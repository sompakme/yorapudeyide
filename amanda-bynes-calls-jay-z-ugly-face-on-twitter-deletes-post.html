Amanda Bynes Calls Jay-Z &quot;Ugly Face&quot; on Twitter, Deletes Post

Amanda Bynes apparently has some beef with Jay-Z, but none of her fans can figure out why. The 26-year-old Easy A actress tweeted a insult at the 43-year-old rapper and mogul this week.

"Ugly face," Bynes wrote, captioning a picture of Beyonce's husband wearing large eyeglasses, E! News reports.

The seemingly unprovoked attack against Jay-Z was immediately deleted from her account, but not before some of her followers had a chance to respond. Jay-Z, however, has not commented.

"Wow. Rude Amanda!" one Twitter user wrote. "I wish I knew what your thought process was behind this," another person tweeted in response to her post.
