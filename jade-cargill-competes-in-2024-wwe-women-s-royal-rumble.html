Jade Cargill Competes In 2024 WWE Womens Royal Rumble

Jade Cargill has arrived.

Jade Cargill appeared at WWE Royal Rumble, as she was the twenty-eighth entrant in the match. The buzzworthy star quickly squared off with Becky Lynch, Nia Jax, and other competitors.

Jax had been on a dominant run in the match, but Cargill overpowered her and eliminated the powerhouse.

Cargill also had an electric staredown with Bianca Belair. The former AEW TBS Champion made it to the final three before she was eliminated by Liv Morgan.

Cargill signed with WWE in September 2023. She then made appearances on RAW, SmackDown, and NXT over the next several weeks. She had been absent in recent weeks, so Saturday marked her return to WWE programming.
