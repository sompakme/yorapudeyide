Mikhail Abyzov

Russian businessman Mikhail Abyzov has an estimated net worth of $1.25 billion as of March 2013 according to Forbes. He currently serves as a member of the board of directors of E4 Group, which is considered as one of the biggest engineering companies in Russia.

Mikhail Abyzov was born on June 3, 1972 in Minsk. He is a graduate of Sholokhov Moscow State Open Pedagogical University which is now known as Moscow State Humanitarian University.

Since 1993, Mikhail Abyzov has been working as a top manager for many Russian-based companies, especially in the fuel and power sectors. In July 2007, he became the Chairman of the board of E4 Group. A month later, he was named as Mostotrest's Chairman of the Board of Directors. Mostotrest is considered as the largest construction company in Russia.
