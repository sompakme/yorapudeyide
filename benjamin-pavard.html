Benjamin Pavard is a French professional footballer. He plays as a right-back for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and also plays in the France national team. 

He was born and raised in France. He gives credit to his father and mother for his successful career.

Benjamin is one of the best players from France. He is an extremely hardworking and successful football player. He is known for his amazing skills on the field. 

He started to show interest from a very young age. He started playing football in 2002 when he was just six years old, and he then started his senior career in 2014. He began to play for the national teams in 2015.
