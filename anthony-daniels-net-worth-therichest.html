Anthony Daniels

Birthname Anthony Daniels Birthdate February 21, 1946 Birthplace Salisbury, Wiltshire, England Source of Wealth acting Nationality British Country United Kingdom Marital Status Married

Anthony Daniels is an English actor best known for his role as C-3PO in the main Star Wars trilogies. He lent his body and voice for the golden robot, and has reprised the role for various promotional work such as hosting The Making of Star Wars, Star Wars Connections, and The Science of Star Wars, appearances on The Muppet Show and Sesame Street, an anti-smoking public service announcement, for the Star Wars Holiday Special and in advertising for Star Wars licensed products.

Other C-3PO voice work done by Daniels includes the Christmas in the Stars album, the multiple Walt Disney theme park attractions, Star Tours and Star Tours: The Adventures Continue, the fan film/show Raiders of the Lost Jedi Temple of Doom: A Fan Film of Epic Proportions, and the Star Tours shutdown ceremony as a part of the Disney's Hollywood Studios' "Last Tour to Endor" event at Star Wars Celebration V in Orlando, Florida. He also provided the narration and all character voices for the audio books for Dark Force Rising and The Last Command.

He also had a recurring role on Prime Suspect and was portrayed as a priest in the 1990 British spoof horror film I Bought A Vampire Motorcycle. 

Anthony Daniels was born on February 21, 1946 in Salisbury, Wiltshire, England. Aside from being an actor, he is an Adjunct Professor at Carnegie Mellon University's Entertainment Technology Center. He is married to Agnes Daniels.
