Max Caster Name-Drops Vince McMahon At A1 Wrestling Show

Max Caster sparked some controversy by referencing Vince McMahon at an independent wrestling show.

AEW star Max Caster appeared at the Alpha-1 Wrestling event on February 4. Caster is known for his freestyle raps, which sometimes contain controversial references to current events. Sunday was no exception, as Caster name-dropped Vince McMahon. During his rap, he said, “You wanna try me on the mic? Get a different plan ’cause I’m freaky with the words like I’m Vince McMahon.”

McMahon is currently under investigation for the allegations Janel Grant made in the lawsuit she filed against him on January 25. In the suit, Grant alleged that McMahon and John Laurinaitis engaged in sexual assault and sex trafficking of her, among other offenses. A spokesperson for McMahon said the lawsuit was “replete with lies.” McMahon then resigned from WWE and TKO on January 26.

Max Caster is one-third of the AEW World Trios Champions alongside Anthony Bowens and Billy Gunn. He is also a former AEW World Tag Team Champion.
