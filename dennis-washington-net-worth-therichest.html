Dennis Washington is the co-owner of a controlling interest in a Montana-based company known as Washington Companies and a Canada-based company Seaspan Marine Corporation. Right after his graduation in high school, he started working in a construction company based in Montana and Alaska. At the age of 30, starting with his $30,000 loaned capital and bulldozer, Washington Construction was formed. The company primarily worked on highway contracts. By 1969, the company became the largest contractor across Montana. In the following year, he started exploring the dam construction and mining industry. When he reopened the molybdenum and copper mine in Montana, he discovered that it was such a profitable business. His success led him to expand his empire and invest in marine services, railroads, real estate, aviation and coastal shipping. Dennis Washington has an estimated net worth of $6 billion as of March 2017, according to Forbes.
