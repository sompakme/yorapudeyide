Cody Rhodes To Join AEW Dynamite Commentary, Creates Drinking Game To Celebrate

With All Elite Wrestling currently filming most of its shows without an audience, the need for some charisma everywhere is much higher. With this in mind, Cody Rhodes has officially decided to join in on the commentary for tomorrow’s episode of AEW Dynamite, and has created a drinking game for the fans watching to play along with.

https://twitter.com/AEWonTNT/status/1242591329802006529

Taking to social media, All Elite Wrestling announced the event, and created two simple rules for fans to follow along with. For every time Cody says “Wait a minute…” during his time on commentary, fans take a drink. If he says it more than once in a row, fans chug their drink for five seconds. Needless to say, it should be a night filled with tons of drink, as Rhodes has a knack for dropping that infamous line when he speaks. You can check out the festivities tomorrow night on AEW Dynamite.
