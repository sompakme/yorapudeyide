Robert De Niro's Most Iconic Roles

Robert De Niro has appeared in over 120 films and has portrayed many dynamic personalities. In 1978's The Deer Hunter, the star portrays Michael, one of the several young factory workers. He joins his friends when they enlist to fight in Vietnam. Falling into the hands of the Viet Cong, they are brought to a prison camp and forced to play Russian roulette against each other.
