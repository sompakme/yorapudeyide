Philip DeFranco Biography And Net Worth

Philip DeFranco, born Philip James Franchini Jr. is an American news commentator and YouTube personality.

Philip James DeFranco was born in The Bronx, New York in the United States of America on December 1, 1985.

DeFranco attended the University of South Florida, Asheville–Buncombe Technical Community College, East Carolina University.

He is best known for The Philip DeFranco Show, a news commentary show centered on current events in politics and pop culture, and his channel ‘Philip DeFranco’ has so far garnered 6.3 million subscribers and over a billion views across the platform.

The Youtuber has also been involved in the creation of other successful channels on YouTube, including personal vlog channel Philly D, the YouTube Original news channel SourceFed and others.

DeFranco is married to Lindsay Jordan Doty, a YouTube travel vlogger, and social media influencer.

Philip DeFranco Net Worth

DeFranco is a millionaire in his own strength. Philip DeFranco has an estimated net worth totaling up to $12 million.
